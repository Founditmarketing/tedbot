# Ted's Knowledge — Raw Source Material

Drop raw interview transcripts here (any format: `.txt`, `.md`, messy is fine).

These files are the **source**. They are distilled down into the tight briefing in
[`services/tedKnowledge.ts`](../../services/tedKnowledge.ts), which is what actually
gets injected into Ted's brain on every chat.

Workflow:
1. Add/paste transcripts in this folder (e.g. `interview-2024-ted-history.md`).
2. Distill the high-signal material into `services/tedKnowledge.ts`:
   - Ted's voice & favorite phrasing
   - Store history & stories (Est. 1899)
   - Style philosophy (fit, fabric, proportion, occasion)
   - Brand opinions (why we carry each, what to reach for when)
   - The fitting/consultation process
   - Common customer questions & his real answers
3. Keep `tedKnowledge.ts` focused — it's a briefing, not a full transcript.
